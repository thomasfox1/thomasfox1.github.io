Key Players
Claude Giroux

-Giroux is the best player the Flyers have had in many years. He is more of a finesse player and he has all the intangibles of an all-star caliber player. Hopefully he stays around in Philadelphia for a long time.

Shayne Gostisbehere

-New to the team, this defenseman has been making a definite impact on a team where there were not many good defenders. He is able to score the puck and dish out assists as well as covering the best opponent on the opposing team.

Wayne Simmonds

-Simmonds is the Associate Captain of this team. He is a tough, gritty player that does not shy away from hitting or getting in fights with opposing players. He is the personification of the city of Philadelphia and a fan favorite.
